#COLLECTION
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	On the day of blood collection, 20 µl of fresh sodium floride (250 mg/ml, NaF)
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	in water was added to each EDTA containing tube. After mice were deeply
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	anaesthetized, blood was sampled from the right heart ventricle 20 min after DMG
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	gavage and 500 µl were transferred to the prepared EDTA tube. After
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	centrifugation (1,500 g) at 4 °C for 10 min, plasma was snap frozen in liquid
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	nitrogen and stored at -80 °C until analysis.
CO:SAMPLE_TYPE                   	Blood (plasma)
CO:STORAGE_CONDITIONS            	-80℃
#TREATMENT
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	Mice were fed with HFb diet (35% fiber, Cat. C1000 modified, #100213, Altromin),
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	LA diet (30% fat, Cat. C1000 modified, #100212, Altromin) or NC (14% Fat, 27%
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	Protein, 59% Carbohydrates, Cat. #1314M, Altromin) ad libitum. After 2 weeks EAE
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	was induced by immunization with an emulsion of MOG35-55 with complete
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	Freund’s adjuvant (CFA) (Hooke KitTM MOG35-55/CFA Emulsion PTX, EK-2110). The
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	emulsion was injected subcutaneously at two sites in the upper and lower back
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	(0.1 ml/site), followed by two intraperitoneal injections of pertussis toxin
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	(PTX) diluted with ice-cold PBS (100 ng in 0.1 ml/dose). Mice were given DMF (50
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	mg/kg body weight) or vehicle orally by gavage every 12 hours. The treatment was
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	provided until the end of the study (dpi 28).
#SAMPLEPREP
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Thawed aliquots of plasma (50 µL) were subjected to solvent extraction once
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	each with 200 µL of cold methanol/acetone/acetonitrile (1/1/1, v/v/v),
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	containing 2.5 µM Metabolomics Amino Acid Mix Standard (Cambridge Isotope
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Laboratories, Andover, MA, United States) and mixed for 15 min at 4°C at 1000
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	rpm (Thermomixer Eppendorf), vortexed for 10 secs, and afterwards incubated for
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	2 h at 20°C. The mixture was then centrifuged for 10 min at 14,000 rpm at 4°C.
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	The collected supernatants were evaporated to dryness in a vacuum concentrator
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	(SpeedVac Concentrator, ThermoFisher). The dry extracts were reconstituted in 25
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	µL of methanol/acetonitrile (1:1) and vortexed for 15 sec followed by
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	centrifugation at 14,000 rpm for 10 min at 4°C. The supernatants were
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	transferred to LC-MS vials, and LC-HRMS analysis was performed.
SP:EXTRACT_STORAGE               	4℃
